Our Verification Process

We maintain the highest standards to ensure quality and trust in our marketplace

1

Initial Application

Complete our detailed application form with information about your breeding program, facilities, and experience. This helps us understand your operation and ensure it meets our standards.

2

Facility Review

Our team will review your facilities through photos, videos, and possibly a virtual tour. We look for clean, humane conditions that prioritize animal welfare.

3

Health & Breeding Records

Submit health records, vaccination history, and breeding documentation. We verify all documentation with your veterinarian and breeding associations.

4

Reference Check

We'll contact your professional references, including veterinarians, other breeders, and potentially previous buyers to verify your reputation and practices.
